History...

Pokemon is around 26 years old at this point and is still among the most popular
and well known brands around the world. The idea was originally created by two men
Sashoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori. Before Pokemon these two men created magazines and video games
with the company Game Freak. After a few years he came up with the idea Pocket Monsters
now known as Pokemon. Pokemon was first released in february 1996 with its original name Pocket Monsters. It was extremely successful selling tons of copies within only Japan.
It was later released in the US and Uk a few years later. Now its selling games, shows, movies and Pokemon cards in extreme numbers. We all remember collecting, playing or watching as a kid, and now you understand some of the backstory for Pocket Monsters the original Pokemon.
